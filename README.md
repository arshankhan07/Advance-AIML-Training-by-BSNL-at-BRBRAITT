# Advance-AIML-Training-by-BSNL-at-BRBRAITT
Advanced AI/ML Training – BSNL & Nokia

A 5-day Advanced AI/ML Training Program organized by BSNL in collaboration with Nokia at BRBRAITT under the Telecom Sector Skill Council.

The program provided valuable insights into Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning concepts, along with practical exposure to their applications in telecom and data-driven environments. It was a great opportunity to understand the working of telecom systems and gain hands-on experience with real-world implementations.

I would like to sincerely thank our trainers Ishan Marwah and Vishwajeet Chatterjee for their guidance and insightful sessions throughout the program.

Overall, it was an enriching and industry-relevant learning experience, and I appreciate BSNL and Nokia for organizing this valuable training initiative.
